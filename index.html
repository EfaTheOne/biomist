The Problem

Traditional pesticides are a blanket solution to a specific problem. They kill everythingâ€”pests, helpful predators, and essential pollinators like bees and butterflies.

This overuse causes severe soil degradation as chemicals seep into the ground, and creates a cycle of dependency. It harms beneficial insects that farmers rely on, and poses long-term risks to the environment.

Current Issues

  • Harmful to pollinators (bees/butterflies)
  • Toxic runoff into water sources
  • High labor costs for application
  • Pest resistance buildup
  • Short-term relief, long-term damage

Our Mission

"Our goal as a company is to help support farmers all over the world starting with Canada."

We want to first reduce the amount of harmful pesticides that are used by farms. This would lower the rates of unhealthy chemicals in food but also in ecosystems from run-off. Secondly we will spread worldwide in order to help not only farmers but everyone around the world have healthier crops, food and lifestyle.

The Solution: BioMist

BioMist is the cure to pesticide overuse. We use synthetic, biodegradable pheromones (semiochemicals) that disrupt pest mating cycles. This creates a huge drop in the population of the next generation of pests without killing them directly or harming the ecosystem.

Our automated dispensing poles are lightweight, sensor-activated, and target only specific pests. Pollinators, predators, and closely related species are left completely unharmed. "BioMist thinks for the farmer and nature."
